Tofu is low in calories and cholesterol-free, promoting heart health and weight management. Adding turmeric boosts its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. ### Ingredients (for 3 persons):
- 600 grams extra firm tofu, crumbled
- 45 mL olive oil
- 7.5 g turmeric powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions:

1. **Heat the oil**: In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
2. **Add the tofu**: Stir in the crumbled tofu.
3. **Season**: Add turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
4. **Cook**: SautÃ© for about 5-7 minutes until the tofu is heated through and slightly golden.
